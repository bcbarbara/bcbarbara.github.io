Research Areas

My research focuses on optimization-based motion planning applied to aerial systems. I’ve dabbled with numerical optimization using CMA-ES incorporating it into modern control theory. I've been involved in embedded numerical optimization applied to a quadrotors at the Systems Control and Optimization Laboratory (syscop), Freiburg, Germany. I've been working with embedded NMPC for quadcopters and optimization-based shared control at the Robotics and InteractionS (RIS) team at LAAS-CNRS, Toulouse, France.
